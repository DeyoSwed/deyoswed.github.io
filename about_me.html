Introduction

A few years back, my friend presented a challenge to rank our top 10 video games of all time, easy right, wrong!
He finishes in one hour, but he doesn't realize everything that needs to be factored into this decision: first time feeling, replayability, hours spent, satisfaction, revolutionary to genre… Yeah, you get the point.
All values were weighted accordingly to finalize the top 10, so three days later, we compared our list, and as a result: we no longer do top 10's together.

My name is Kristofer, and I'm 33 years old. After a few too many experiences like the one above, I have finally given in and accepted my inclination towards data driven decision making, from working primarily with strategy, leadership, and retail/sales education, to complementing my previous experiences by entering academia in the field of data science.

Ohh. and I also like traveling and dancing!
